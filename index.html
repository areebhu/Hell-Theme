Background Story

Cerberus is a three-headed beast that guards the entrance of the hell and now he’s back to guard our
Greek museum in the Metaverse. Our plan is to build a Greek museum where we all love Greek Gods and
Goddesses. In that Metaverse, you can come to hang around with your unique NFTs which are
Cerberus666. 30% of the profit will go towards a charity chosen by our NFT owners and 70% of the
revenue will go towards the development of Metaverse. The owners of Cerberus666 will share the
revenue generated by Greek Museum from the visitors who come to know the truth about The Great
Greeks. So, be a part of our dope Greek Museum gang.
